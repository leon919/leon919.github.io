This website allows you to record their items you would like to buy but not yet ready to go shopping. This website is generally for people like you who have busy life. If you are running out of supplies at home but not ready to go out to shop then the you could use this tool to record the items that are missing. Once you are ready to go shopping, you can bring the list to the supermarket or use the list while shopping online. The tool is available on mobile app.
